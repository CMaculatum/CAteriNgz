About Us

Learn about CAteriNgz!

CAteriNgz began its journey in a modest kitchen and has since flourished into a premier catering service renowned for providing exceptional food at events in Jakarta, Bandung, and Malang. Our story is one of passion, dedication, and a deep love for culinary arts. We specialize in crafting memorable culinary experiences tailored to a wide array of gatherings, including weddings, corporate events, family reunions, and more. Each event we cater is treated with the utmost care and attention to detail, ensuring that every dish not only satisfies but also delights our clients and their guests.

Our commitment to quality is unwavering. We take pride in sourcing our ingredients locally, supporting local farmers and artisans. By focusing on seasonal produce, we ensure that our dishes are not only fresh and flavorful but also sustainable and environmentally friendly. Our chefs work hand-in-hand with local suppliers to select the best ingredients, bringing the finest flavors to your table. This approach not only guarantees the highest quality but also helps to sustain the local economy and reduce our environmental footprint.

Our culinary team is comprised of skilled chefs who bring a wealth of experience and creativity to the kitchen. They are dedicated to continuously innovating and perfecting our menu, combining traditional techniques with contemporary trends to create dishes that are both classic and modern. Beyond the food, we believe that exceptional service is key to a successful event. Our professional staff is trained to provide a seamless and enjoyable experience for our clients, from the initial consultation to the final bite of dessert. We listen to your needs and work tirelessly to exceed your expectations, ensuring that every aspect of your event reflects your unique vision.
